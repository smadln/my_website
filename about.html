Sarah Madeline

about

Sarah Madeline is a Canadian artist and writer based in Aliso Viejo, California. After graduating from her BFA, she became Head Curator at The Sunroom Artspace in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Treaty 1 territory. Madeline was a senior editor and writer for SCAN, a contemporary art publication at the University of Manitoba. She has since moved to California to continue her practice as a visual artist.

Madeline`s work sifts through new and obsolete technology to fuse the distinct qualities of each to one another. Her use of analog and digital processes reflects on flux and temporality, which are key elements in her practice. Sound, image, and natural materials are often incorporated in her work to synthesize physical and virtual spaces. Each medium is utilized to harness a balance between analyzing the past and anticipating the future.
