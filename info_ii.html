Sarah Madeline

Sarah Madeline is a Canadian artist, writer, and researcher based in Southern California, United States. Madeline was chief editor and contributing writer for SCAN, a contemporary art publication at the University of Manitoba. She later became head curator at The Sunroom Artspace in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Treaty 1 territory. She has since moved to California to continue her practice.

Through browser-based and born-digital archival work, Madeline strives to harness a balance between examining the past and anticipating the future.
